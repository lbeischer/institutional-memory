## How to Get Read-Only Prod Access

### ⚠️ First: Check your tenure
You must have **at least 3 working days** at the company before access can be granted (i.e., you're eligible from **day 4**). If you literally started today or yesterday, this is the hard blocker — the policy has no exception path documented for new joiners under this threshold.

---

### If you've hit 3 working days, here are the steps:

**1. Complete the "Prod Access Foundations" course**
- Go to the **BTS Learning portal** and find the *Prod Access Foundations* course.
- It's self-paced, ~90 minutes. Complete it and pass the assessment.

**2. Get your manager's sign-off**
- Manager approval is required **once** — for the initial certification. This is the only person you need to involve.
- Loop in your manager now so they're ready to approve as soon as you finish the course.

**3. Request access via the IAM platform**
- After passing the assessment, you'll receive a **completion email with a link to the IAM platform**.
- Submit your access request there. Access is granted **just-in-time** and scoped to a **4-hour window**.
- You can re-request as many times as you need — each new window just requires a new IAM request (no re-approval from your manager after the initial certification).
